About

Let me introduce myself.

My name is Akram ジ. I am a 22 year old guy who lives in Agadir,Morocco. My designing journey began when I first intended to learn designing as a hobby, then decided to join a school to professionally grasp it. One thing led to another and 3 years went by and I was a fully fledged freelance graphic designer. This fortunate turn of events has lead me to work as an intern for Grafimage and design for some cool clients like "AllSoussMassa", "Oasis Atlantico Saïdia" and many more. I have been a graphic designer for a while and it has given me more love for this work.
